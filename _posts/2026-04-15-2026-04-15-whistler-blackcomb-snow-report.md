It was sunny and cold after 7 cm of overnight snow, making for a bluebird powder day.

Off-piste conditions were not great, with dust on crust, but the groomers were pretty good and it was not too busy. Upper Peak To Creek was not the best, even though it should have been fully groomed.

Overall rate: 4/5 ★★★★☆
