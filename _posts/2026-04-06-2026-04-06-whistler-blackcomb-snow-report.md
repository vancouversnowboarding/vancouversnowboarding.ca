Warm and mostly sunny with a thin layer of cloud; visibility held up through midday despite glare, and Upper Olympic at 10am was the only good window before and after the light shifted.

Snow was inconsistent, icy at first and gradually soft and unstable; Chick Pea Hut had the garage door wide open and the cinnamon buns smelled great, but multiple race events kept the runs unsafe and the hill very busy.

Overall rate: 3/5 ★★★☆☆
