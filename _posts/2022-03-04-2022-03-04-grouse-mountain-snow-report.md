It's clear sunny but not too hot, and the snow is very soft today. Patches of fog occasionary hit open areas but they dissapear very quickly. The visibility has been great.

Overall rate: 4/5 ★★★★☆

Closed runs:

* Coffin
* Peak Glades
* Lower Blazes
