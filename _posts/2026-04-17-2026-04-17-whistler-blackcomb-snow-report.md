It stayed cold since the last powder day, with no overnight snow.

View from Symphony Express unload
![](/assets/images/2026-04-17-view-from-symphony-express-unload.jpg)

The groomers were the best of the season. Snow was firm with a soft top layer, with excellent grip that was not sticky or unstable, so it was ideal for carving. It was also the last day of the Whistler mountain side.

Overall rate: 5/5 ★★★★★
