9cm overnight gave the upper mountain a decent reset, but it only felt like about 1cm by the lower half of Big Red, especially around Pony Trail.

The bowls were the best riding of the day, with soft snow on top and much better flow once you got into the open terrain. There were still occasional icy avi debris patches underneath, so it paid to stay alert and keep a light edge.

Visibility was low at the ridges and peaks, but it was fine everywhere else. Chairs were not that busy overall, though Symphony got busier later in the day.

Overall rate: 4/5 ★★★★☆
