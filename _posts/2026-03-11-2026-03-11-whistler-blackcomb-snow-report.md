3 cm of overnight snow fell, which made the surface better than our [Tuesday, March 10](/2026-03-10-2026-03-10-whistler-blackcomb-snow-report.md/) run two days earlier, but everything still felt packed and clung to a firm base.

It was -11C on the mountain, so the air stayed nice and cold while the groomers stayed hard and clipped in despite the light fresh layer.

Visibility was solid from the mid-station down through the lower mountain, but the alpine lost that clarity and the off-piste terrain never opened up — it simply was not good today.

Overall rate: 3/5 ★★★☆☆
