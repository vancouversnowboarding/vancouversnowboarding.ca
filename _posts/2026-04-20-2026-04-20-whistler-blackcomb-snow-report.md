Blackcomb-only day, with the resort opening at 9:00am this week instead of 8:30am. It was a hot, sunny, 11C slushy day.

Mainline
![](/assets/images/2026-04-20-mainline.jpg)

The snow was stable inside and soft on top, with good grip. There was not enough snow at mid to bottom, and the Blackcomb gondola was not connected from parking lot 6.

Overall rate: 3/5 ★★★☆☆
