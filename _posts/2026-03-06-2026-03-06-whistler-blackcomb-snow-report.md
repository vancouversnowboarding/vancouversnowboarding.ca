It was another great day. Little bit of fresh snow during overnight made the groomers really good, and the resort opened fairly early so it was not very busy even though it was Friday. The snow was really good everywhere.

The visibility was not the best, but clear at the mid mountain, such as from the top to the bottom of Emerald 6 or the most of Big Red Express. Alpine was completely flat light and not recommended to go there, though the snow was even better.

Overall rate: 4/5 ★★★★☆
