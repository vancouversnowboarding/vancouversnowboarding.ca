Partly sunny skies this morning followed the 9cm of overnight snow, keeping visibility mostly clear except for brief fog around the alpine bowls; Big Red Express stopped frequently but the rest of the upper mountain maintained steady service.  

Snow quality stayed soft and supportive underfoot, with only a short wait before Harmony 6 Express finally opened and a few Big Red pauses; overall it was not very busy, so laps were easy to string together even with those hiccups.

Overall rate: 5/5 ★★★★★
